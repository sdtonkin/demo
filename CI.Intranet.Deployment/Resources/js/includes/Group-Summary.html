    Free Book Friday

    Clifford E. Graese Community Health Library

    5:00pm est

    To celebrate National Medical Librarian’s Month, the Library Services team will be having the annual Free Book Friday on Friday October 27. Come to the Clifford E. Graese Community Health Library behind ORMC for free books, refreshments and treats, and learn more about the great resources and services available to you from our libraries and Library Services team, some which are listed below.

    Want to donate books to Free Book Friday? Please bring any donations of gently used fiction, non-fiction, textbooks, or children’s books to library before Friday, October 27.
